## 例文

While the transition to renewable energy is widely recognized as an existential imperative, the inherent volatility of global markets and the reliance on legacy infrastructure continue to impede progress, illustrating the immense difficulty of decarbonizing the economy without compromising energy security.

## 単語

### imperative

- 必須の、緊急の、命令的

### inherent

- 固有の

### volatility

- 興奮しやすいこと、優柔不断である特性

### reliance

- 信頼、頼ること

### impade

- 妨げる

### immense

- 巨大な、計り知れない

### compromise

- 歩み寄り、和解、示談
