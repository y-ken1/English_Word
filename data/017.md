## 例文

During a turbulent period marked by political repression, the government tended to overstate the threat it faced, creating a surreal atmosphere in which citizens were expected to tolerate the expropriate of property, even as officials claimed that disputes were resolved amicably.

## 単語

### turbulent

- 激動の、混乱した、荒れた

### repression

- 抑圧、弾圧

### surreal

- 非現実的な、現実離れした

### amicably

- 友好的に、円満に

### tolerate

- 容認する、我慢する

### expropriate

- 収用する、強制的に取り上げる
