## 例文

The rebel group challenged the predominance of the central authority within its jurisdiction, distributing a brochure that promised to reimburse displaced residents, while warning that economic deceleration had left the once pristine valley strewn with abandoned equipment after an awe-inspiring yet dangerous experiment involving nuclear irradiation.

## 単語

### predominance

- 優勢、支配的な影響力

### brochure

- パンフレット、小冊子

### deceleration

- 減速、減速化

### strew

- まき散らす、散在させる

### pristine

- 手つかずの、汚れのない

### awe-inspiring

- 畏敬の念を起こさせる、圧倒的な

### irradiation

- 放射（線）、照射
